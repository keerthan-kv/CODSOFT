Games Overview

Need for Speed: Porsche Unleashed (2000)

A unique installment focusing on the legacy of Porsche with realistic car physics and an immersive career mode.

Need for Speed: Underground 2 (2004)

Dive into the world of street racing with extensive customization options and an open-world environment.

Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005)

Outrun the cops in high-speed pursuits while building your reputation in this iconic racer.

Need for Speed: Rivals (2013)

Choose your side as a cop or racer in this intense open-world battle for dominance.

Need for Speed: No Limits (2015)

Conquer the streets with custom cars and high-speed racing action, optimized for mobile gaming.

Need for Speed: Payback (2017)

Embark on a story-driven adventure with explosive missions and fast-paced action in the city of Fortune Valley.

Need for Speed: Heat (2019)

Race by day and risk it all by night in this electrifying battle for street racing supremacy.

Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit Remastered (2020)

Relive the thrilling cop vs. racer chases with enhanced visuals and cross-platform multiplayer.

Need for Speed Unbound (2022)

Push the limits of speed and style in a visually unique world with a fresh take on street racing.
